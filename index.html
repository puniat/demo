Correlation Between Education Level and Income

This project aims to analyze the correlation between education levels and income using a Bayesian regression model. It visualizes income data against various education levels to explore the common belief that higher education correlates strongly with higher income.

Graph will appear here (Python output)

Education levels may not influence income as significantly as commonly believed. Data for this analysis is sourced from the Kaggle dataset “Income Data,” which includes income, education level, age, race, sex, and more. Although higher education can correlate with higher income in some cases, the relationship is not always strong or consistent.
